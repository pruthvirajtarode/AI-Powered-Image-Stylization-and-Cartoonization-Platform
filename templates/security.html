Data Protection

How do you protect my photos?

Images are processed in an isolated RAM-based buffer. We use AES-256 encryption for any temporary storage, and files are auto-purged every 24 hours.

What data do you get from Google?

We only receive your verified email and name. We never request access to your Drive, Contacts, or any other private Google assets. Our integration is read-only for identity.

Is the AI processing local or cloud?

To ensure high performance, processing happens on secure, SOC2-compliant cloud clusters. All data transmission is protected by a solid SSL/TLS 1.3 handshake.

Popular Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All original and processed images are encrypted and automatically deleted from our cloud servers after 24 hours.
