Privacy Policy

Transparency is our first principle. Learn how we protect your digital identity.

Our Commitment

At Toonify AI, your privacy isn't just a checkbox—it's the foundation of everything we build. We process millions of artistic transformations daily, and we treat every single image with the same rigorous security standards.

How We Handle Your Data

  • Neural-Only Processing: Images are processed in a volatile RAM buffer. Once the transformation is complete, the original raw data is purged from the buffer.
  • 24-Hour Auto-Delete: Processed images are stored in an encrypted cloud bucket for 24 hours to allow for user downloads, after which they are permanently deleted.
  • No Face Recognition: Our neural networks analyze geometry for stylization—we never store facial biometric data or use it for identification.
  • Third-Party Security: We use official Google OAuth for identity management, meaning we never see or store your Google password.

Your Rights

You have the absolute right to request a full dump of your account data or an immediate account deletion. To exercise these rights, simply visit your Security Dashboard or contact our privacy officer at privacy@toonify.ai.
