Popular Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All original and processed images are encrypted and automatically deleted from our cloud servers after 24 hours.

Can I use the images commercially?

Pro and Elite members receive a full commercial license for all generated artworks.

How does the Google Login work?

We use the official Google OAuth 2.0 protocol. We never see your password; Google only sends us a secure "Digital Stamp" of your identity.
