2. **Energy**: Start at 7/10, peak at 9/10 during demo, end at 8/10
3. **Eye Contact**: Camera or audience - make connection
4. **Pauses**: Silence is powerful. Use it.
5. **Gestures**: Point, guide attention, be natural
6. **Enthusiasm**: Show genuine excitement about the product
7. **Confidence**: If you stumble, keep going - no apologies
