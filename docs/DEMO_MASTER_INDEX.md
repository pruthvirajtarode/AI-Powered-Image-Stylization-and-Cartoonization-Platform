## 💡 PRO TIPS FROM EXPERIENCED PRESENTERS

### Before Presenting
- Practice in front of mirror
- Record yourself and watch (painful but effective)
- Present to a friend and ask for feedback
- Time yourself multiple times
- Prepare 5 variations (in case of issues)
- Drink water beforehand
- Do breathing exercises (calms nerves)

### During Presenting
- Arrive 15 minutes early
- Smile (trust me, it changes your voice)
- Speak slower than feels natural
- Pause frequently (especially after big reveals)
- Use hand movements (guides attention)
- Make eye contact (camera or audience)
- If you mess up, keep going (no apologies)
- Energy > Perfection (always)

### After Presenting
- Thank people immediately
- Collect emails/contact info
- Share follow-up within 24 hours
- Ask for feedback
- Document learnings
- Celebrate the team
- Plan improvements
